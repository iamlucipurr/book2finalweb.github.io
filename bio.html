About us!

In the past year, Zeke Stevens and Kat Fechner (GRIM & REAPER) have made quite the impact on the local electronic music scene. Masterfully blending genres such as swing and jazz into powerful EDM sets to bring a little class and nostalgia to whatever venue they may be playing. With an unofficial residency at one of SLC’s underground clubs, they have begun to expose SLC’s rave scene to the otherwise unknown genre of Swing-Electro.

Starting out in the fall of 2012, they have already became regular DJ’s at a series of underground events, opened for multiple big name acts, and released a 5 track EP (6 tracks if you get the CD from them in person). Their production style knows no bounds, dabbling in a range of genres from Glitch Hop, Electro, Moombahcore, Drum & Bass, Dubstep, and even a touch of Industrial; most of which still in the works. Expect a multitude of songs to be released in the following few months.

In the summer of 2013, Grim & Reaper was approached by Ian of Nova Lotus Records, an online label based out of Salt Lake City, and was quickly welcomed into the family. Collaborations with Nova Lotus artists are in production and highly anticipated.
